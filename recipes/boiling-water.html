Boiling Water

Description

Do you need to make pasta? Maybe you're under a boil water advisory? Or perhaps you'd like throw it up into freezing air to see what happens.

Ingredients

  • pot sized to hold the amount of water you need boiled
  • water

Steps

  1. Begin with a clean, empty pot
  2. Fill it with water from your sink
  3. While holding the pot, ask yourself why you know how to google things, but not how to boil water
  4. Lose track of time while pondering this question and fill the pot up until it is unnecessarily full
  5. Struggle to lift the pot out of the sink and over to the stove without spilling any water
  6. Turn the heat under the pot to high
  7. Realize that a watched pot never boils, but that it will be difficult to know if the water is boiling without being able to see it
  8. Decide to avoid this problem by simply standing there and waiting to hear the water boil
  9. Place a lid on the pot to decrease the time needed to boil
