Ice Cubes

Description

They're cold, they're crunchy, and they can chill a beverage. No freezer is complete without ice cubes in it!

Ingredients

  • ice cube tray
  • water

Steps

  1. Begin with an empty ice cube tray
  2. Fill it with water from your sink
  3. While holding the tray, wonder about the quality of your tap water
  4. Begin to worry about the prevalence of microplastics in the world today, as well as the negative health consequences of ingesting them
  5. Consider buying an expensive water filtration system
  6. Realize that filtration system would likely have plastic components, too
  7. Decide that you'll just try not to think too much about it
  8. Place the filled ice cube tray in the freezer
  9. After three to four hours, enjoy your ice cubes!
