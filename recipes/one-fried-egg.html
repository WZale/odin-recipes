One Fried Egg

Description

Do you have barely any appetite at all? Is frying an egg your idea of gourmet cooking? Or do you just want to start small?

Ingredients

  • a seasoned cast-iron skillet
  • one egg
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Begin with a clean skillet or frying pan
  2. Contemplate how a non-stick skillet would probably make this easier, but that teflon likely contains carcinogenic forever chemicals
  3. Grease the pan with a good amount of olive oil
  4. Turn the heat up to medium
  5. Wait for the oil to shimmer
  6. Wonder what "shimmering" oil even looks like, really
  7. Crack the egg into the oil
  8. For sunny-side-up eggs, simply wait 3-5 minutes, or until the yolk is just slightly runny
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  10. Scrape the egg out of the pan, and realize that your cast-iron likely needs to be re-seasoned
